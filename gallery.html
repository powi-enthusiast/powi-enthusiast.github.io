About Me

Just starting through in journalism, I have prior experience as a reporter as well as in editing local newspapers and online news portals. My main interests are international politics and economics. I strive to provide valuable information to my audience and to tell stories that matter.

I'm still completing a post-grad degree in political science at the moment. Through my research I have been able to specialize in international order politics and conflict analysis. Within this field especially in power politics, imperialism and cyber conflict analysis.

My other big interest is photography, which I like to utilize to improve my storytelling via a multimedial approach.
